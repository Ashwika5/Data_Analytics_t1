# Data_Analytics_t1
📋 Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive business intelligence analysis covering 4 years of sales data (2011-2014). The analysis provides actionable insights into regional performance, category profitability, discount impact, and seasonal trends to support strategic decision-making.

🎯 Key Objectives
Identify top-performing regions and product categories

Analyze the relationship between discounts and profitability

Uncover seasonal patterns and monthly performance trends

Provide data-driven recommendations for management

Highlight top customer segments for retention strategies

📈 Key Findings Summary
Metric	Value
Total Sales	$12.64M
Total Profit	$1.47M
Total Quantity Sold	178K units
Profit Margin	~11.6%
Best Performing Region	South ($1.16M)
Most Profitable Category	Technology (37.53% of sales)
Best Month	December ($1.58M)
Top Customer	Tamara Chand ($8,672.90 profit)
📌 Quick Insights
✅ Strong Growth: Sales nearly doubled from $2.25M (2011) to $4.3M (2014)

⚠️ Margin Concern: Profit margin at 11.6% indicates room for improvement

📈 Seasonal Peak: Q4 (October-December) consistently outperforms

🏆 Regional Leader: South region significantly outperforms others
